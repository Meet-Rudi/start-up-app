Who is Rudi?

Rudi is a digital health coach, developed by general practitioner Dr. Frederik Dierick and clinical psychologist Beata Bouwer. Over 90 days, Rudi guides you via WhatsApp towards eating healthier, moving more and sleeping better — with small, achievable steps that fit your life.

The big difference from an app: Rudi doesn't wait until you ask something. Rudi sends you messages himself, encourages you, thinks along with you when things aren't going well, and adapts the coaching to your pace. You can think of him as a health coach in your pocket, with endless knowledge and motivation to work on your health together with you.

Good to know:

  • Rudi does not replace your doctor and gives no advice about medication. For medical questions, your GP or specialist remains your point of contact.
  • This is a pilot project. Your experience helps us improve Rudi further. That's why we ask you a few short questions now, and the same ones again after 90 days. That way we can see your progress together.
  • You can stop at any time, without giving a reason.

Step 3 of 3

Your data is in safe hands

  • Everything you fill in here and everything you later discuss with Rudi is encrypted and stored securely on servers within the European Union.
  • Your data is never sold and never used for advertising purposes and it will be deleted after the pilot project completion in line with privacy policy.
  • CM does not receive individual answers or conversations — only anonymous group results about the pilot project.
  • You can view, correct or delete your data at any time. One email to privacy@meetrudi.eu is all it takes.
  • You can stop at any time: just send "STOP" to Rudi. Withdrawing your consent is as easy as giving it.
This consent is required to take part.

After you register, Rudi will reach out as soon as the pilot project begins. If something goes wrong or you have questions, send an email to support@meetrudi.eu.
