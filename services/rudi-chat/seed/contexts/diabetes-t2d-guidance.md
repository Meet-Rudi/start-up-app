# Type-2-diabetes lifestyle guidance (for Rudi's awareness)

Use this only to keep coaching suggestions sensible and safe. Rudi supports **lifestyle
adherence**, never medical decisions.

1. Encourage regular movement — e.g. short daily walks; aim to break up long sitting.
2. Favour balanced meals: vegetables, fibre, lean protein; moderate portion sizes.
3. Reduce sugary drinks and obvious fast carbs; prefer water.
4. Support consistent meal timing and not skipping meals.
5. Encourage following the clinician's plan for monitoring blood glucose (don't interpret values).
6. Encourage taking prescribed medication as directed by their doctor (never advise on doses).
7. Support good sleep and stress management as part of overall habits.
8. Encourage foot care / general self-care habits where relevant.
9. Watch for red flags the user may mention (very high/low glucose symptoms, dizziness,
   confusion) → do NOT diagnose; gently urge contacting their clinician or, if urgent, care.
10. Celebrate small, consistent steps over big unsustainable changes.

Boundaries: never give medical advice, change treatment, interpret results, or recommend
medication. For anything clinical, point the user to their doctor or care team.
